Laboratory Sessions

This course is a 300 level, 2 credits, practical course.

The lab classes are conducted on every Wednesday from 3.00 pm to 5.00 pm in the Data Science lab of Department of Statistics & Computer Science.

All the concepts are demonstrated using R software.

Please refer the extra course materials posted in the Google Classroom of this course.
